Protocolo Tici:
.1         Question Focus:
The development and enhancement of cognitive skills in people who are blind has been highly demanded in the literature. Digital technology targeting this need is widely desired and demanded. There is also a need beyond the technology development. Usability studies of technology for people with visual disability are claimed and required for better understanding and adequate and pertinent use. This proposal is targeting this need and demand. We focus on Knowing and Interacting while Gaming for the Blind, KIGB, whose expected result is a multimodal videogame application for enhancing cognition in people with visual disabilities.
 
1.2 Question Quality and Amplitude
a) Problem: To identify appropriate approaches for developing mental models, cognitive spatial structures, and navigation skills in blind learners through videogames; as well as to verify wich are the most suitable approaches and technologies for multimodal and usability evaluation in this context.
b) Questions:
Q1 - What strategies have been used for the design of multimodal games for blind learners in order to enhance cognition?
 
Q2 - What strategies have been used to evaluate usability and quality of multimodal games for blind learners?
 
Q3 - What technologies have been used for the development of multimodal games for blind learners, in order to enhance cognition?
 
* In this context, enhance cognition means to help Developing mental models, cognitive spatial structures, and navigation skills

c) Keywords and Synonyms:
·         Multimodal videogames
·         Blind learners
·         Mental Models
·         Cognitive spatial structures
·         Navigation Skills
Multimodal evaluation methods
